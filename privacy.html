Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

EasyTunnel is private by design. The short version: there are no accounts, no telemetry, and no data collection of any kind — nothing leaves your device.

No accounts, no data collection

EasyTunnel does not require an account and does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There is no analytics or telemetry. We don't operate any servers that your app talks to.

Your credentials stay on your device

SSH passwords and private keys you save are stored in the iOS Keychain on your device (protected and not synced to iCloud). They're used only to connect to the servers you configure, and are never sent to us or any third party.

Network connections

EasyTunnel connects only to the SSH servers and forwarded ports that you configure. The in-app browser loads only the addresses you enter — typically your own forwarded localhost ports. Host-key fingerprints are pinned locally so you're warned if a server's identity changes.

Camera & microphone

If a web app you open in EasyTunnel's in-app browser requests the camera or microphone, iOS asks your permission first — exactly as Safari does. EasyTunnel itself does not access your camera or microphone.

Children's privacy

EasyTunnel does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
